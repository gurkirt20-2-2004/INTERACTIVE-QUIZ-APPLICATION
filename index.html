About This Quiz App

This immersive quiz app is designed to help you learn and test your knowledge across multiple topics. Features include dynamic question loading, instant feedback, detailed explanations, and result tracking. Your scores are saved locally so you can track your progress over time.

Built with modern web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), it offers smooth animations and an interactive UI. Whether you're a beginner or advanced learner, this app adapts to your pace.
